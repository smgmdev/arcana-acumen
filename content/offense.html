Capabilities/Offense

Offense

Arcana Mace connects to ready interceptors and fires only on human confirmation. Defense analytics inform the decision — offense action is always commander-authorised.

Analytics first, engagement second

The engagement path is deliberately sequential: the system first delivers real-time defense analytics; the operator reviews the target, source, weapon class, and trajectory; only then is offense action authorised. This is an architectural rule, not a configurable setting.

Human-in-the-loop, always

No interception is initiated without explicit commander authorisation. AI provides targeting solutions — humans authorise engagement.

Engagement path

Detect

Threat crosses a protection zone

Alert card fires with weapon type, source, target, and trajectory.

Track

Live tracking opens

Live ETA, lat/lon position, engagement-theatre map feed, and nearest interceptor in range.

Confirm

Commander authorises

Operator selects the connected interceptor and confirms engagement — the system does not proceed otherwise.

Eliminate

Target neutralised

Interceptor engages the moving target, live countdown runs, trajectory is removed, and the alert card flips to TARGET ELIMINATED.
