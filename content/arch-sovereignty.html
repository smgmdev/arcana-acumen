Local government Azure region — facts

Physical

Physical location: local government capital. Local-sovereign cloud designation. Data-residency guarantee in contract. Geo-redundancy: secondary local region. 99.99% SLA on core services.

Legal & compliance

ISO 27001 / SOC 2 certified. Local national cyber-security authority compliance capable. No foreign CLOUD Act jurisdiction applies.

Classified feeds

Commercial satellite OSINT data (unclassified) flows through cloud normally. Any data provided by government (classified feeds, additional sensor data) is processed only on isolated, air-gapped government-controlled infrastructure that Arcana Mace deploys on-premise — it never touches public cloud.
