Capabilities/Global procurement

Global production → procurement

Arcana Mace enables cross-government factory procurement. Governments display available factories manufacturing specific products on the global procurement radar — other governments place orders and receive supply in real time.

Global procurement radar

Every producing factory can be exposed as a procurement endpoint. Allied governments browse live inventory by product and region, see available stock counters in real time, and place orders directly against producing factories — no intermediary, no offline paperwork.

Factory listing

Governments list their factories on the global radar with product, stock level, and transport options — visible only to authorised allied governments.

Live order placement

Other governments place orders against live stock. Quantities are capped to factory totals, and stock is live-rechecked at submission so stale orders cannot dispatch.

Real-time supply

On order, dispatch animates across the globe — air, sea, or road — and the receiving country's stockpile updates on arrival with a delivery confirmation.

Sovereign audit trail

Every order, dispatch, and delivery is logged in a sovereign project record — no data leaves the perimeter, every action is replayable.

Order flow

List

Factory production available to subscribed government clients

Producing government authorises a factory for cross-government visibility.

Order

Allied government places order

Destination country, quantity, transport mode selected; stock live-rechecked.

Dispatch

Shipment animates across the globe

Air (high arc), sea (low arc), or road (minimal arc) — plays once on submit.

Deliver

Stockpile credited

Destination country inventory updates, delivery toast fires, and the product enters the recipient's deploy queue.
