Capabilities/Production

Production

Ability to run production in real time through reflecting software to manage global production and assign stocks — live supply tracking and the ability to deploy or assign specific products to specific countries worldwide.

Live factory tracking

Production lines

Factories run multiple product lines simultaneously. Each line ticks independently with live progress bars and stock counters updating in real time.

Inventory tracking in real time

Tracking production inventory real time worldwide. Every country carries a living stockpile that updates as factories deliver, as dispatches arrive, and as units are deployed or recalled.

Aggregated inventory

A single view aggregates every product across every factory and every country — factory sources and country stockpiles broken down side-by-side.

Continuous throughput

Production runs across every open project and mission tab — throughput never pauses while an operator works on another view.

Assign & deploy worldwide

Finished product can be assigned directly to any country on the globe. Dispatch travels by air, sea, or road as an animated shipment on the command picture and credits the destination country's stockpile on arrival. Once stockpiled, products can be deployed onto that country's sovereign territory with a single click.

Product assignment

Specific units — including directed-energy interceptors, drones, missiles, and ammunition — can be assigned and deployed to specific countries in real time. Every assignment carries provenance, recall eligibility, and full audit trail.
