Capabilities/Defense

Defense

Arcana Mace supports defense with real-time AI analytics across space intelligence and ground-sensor systems — classifying drones, missiles, and other threats the moment they appear.

Inputs — space & ground

Space intelligence

Commercial satellite imagery, RF signals intelligence, and AIS maritime feeds provide a persistent overhead picture across the region of interest.

Ground sensors

Physical drone-detection and radar nodes deployed at critical infrastructure — airports, oil facilities, government sites, coastline, borders — feed live sensor data into the analytics layer.

Real-time AI analytics

Incoming streams are run through the AI layer continuously — classifying threat category, weapon type, source, and target. Operators see the full picture on a live 3D globe with colour-coded trajectories, alert cards, and per-threat tracking.

What the system detects

Drones, UAVs, ballistic and cruise missiles, maritime intrusions, aerial incursions, and signals-intelligence anomalies — each classified with weapon type, source, target, and confidence score in real time.

Protection zones

Operators paint protection zones directly onto the globe — airports, capitals, oil infrastructure, bases. Every live trajectory is continuously evaluated against every zone; the instant a threat crosses a boundary, a THREAT alert card fires with weapon type, source, target, and one-click escalation to tracking and engagement.
