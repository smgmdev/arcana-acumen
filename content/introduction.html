Arcana Mace/Overview/Introduction

Arcana Mace

The first sovereign AI defense platform that sees, thinks, and strikes as one system. Defense, offense, production, procurement, and mission automation — merged into a single live command picture, ready for real-time deployment and cross-government collaboration.

Version 1.0 Updated April 2026 Classification Confidential

Six capabilities, one platform

Arcana Mace is built as a continuous operational loop. Every layer feeds the next — intelligence becomes action, action becomes production, production becomes procurement, procurement becomes deployment, and AI-driven automation improves every step in real time.

Defense

Real-time AI analytics on space intelligence and ground-sensor feeds. Drones, missiles, and signals threats classified and surfaced live.

Offense

Connects to ready interceptors and fires only on human confirmation. Analytics-driven offense, never autonomous engagement.

Production

Live factory production and inventory. Assign and deploy specific products to specific countries anywhere on the globe in real time.

Global procurement

Cross-government procurement radar. Governments list factories, other governments order against live stock — all in one system.

Automation

AI creates modules, runs them, and builds additional modules to close the gap to the user's desired outcome. Applied across mission strategy and production.

Adaptability

Fully adaptable, scalable, and extensible with unlimited add-on features — ground, sea, air — production, defense, and offense. All real time.

The operational loop

Step 01 · Defense

See everything, in real time

Space intelligence and ground-sensor systems feed the AI layer. Threats — drone, missile, and signals — are classified and surfaced live on the command picture.

Step 02 · Offense

Act on analytics, on human command

Connected interceptors remain on standby. Once defense analytics confirm a target, the operator authorises engagement — the system never fires autonomously.

Step 03 · Production

Factories inside the command picture

Factory throughput is tracked live inside the same system. Finished product is assigned and deployed to specific countries worldwide without leaving the workspace.

Step 04 · Procurement

Cross-government marketplace

Governments list producing factories on the global procurement radar. Other governments place orders against live stock and receive supply in real time.

Step 05 · Automation

AI closes the gap to the goal

Users define the desired outcome. AI runs the current modules, identifies what is missing, and builds additional modules to reach the goal — across mission strategy, production, and any other domain.

Step 06 · Adaptability

Extend without limit

New add-on features plug into the platform continuously — from ground to sea to air, from production to defense to offense. Everything, in real time.
